How Teachers Calculate Your Grades

In the Philippines, grades are usually based on three components:

ComponentWeight
Written Works30%
Performance Tasks50%
Quarterly Assessment20%

Your grade is calculated by multiplying each component score by its weight and adding the results.

Essential School Information

  • Bring all necessary materials: notebooks, pens, and ID.
  • Manage your time: make a study schedule and stick to it.
  • Participate actively in class for better understanding.
  • Review lessons regularly to avoid cramming.
  • Take care of your health: sleep well and eat properly.
